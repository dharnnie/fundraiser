About me

My name is Daniel, a student, Technology Enthusiast and a Software Developer. I'm also Nigerian and African

This will take less than 2 minutes

Earlier this year (2017), I got accepted into the African Leadership University (ALU's website) in Mauritius to study Computer Science & Software Engineering. I have been working hard to raise funds ever since but it has not been coming along as I expected.

Typically, I require $15,000 every year (for 4 years) to study at ALU (African Leadership University). Currently, I have received support that is equivalent to $4000 every year for 3 years from a Leadership program I attended a year back. This amount equates to $12,000.
I am deeply passionate about Technology and the future of Africa with regards to Technological growth and development and that was what spurred my decision to study at African Leadership University (ALU).

ALU provides a unique learning model and experience. One of the most enthralling values ALU provide is a mandatory 4 month (yearly) internship that has been fused into their learning model. They also provide the opportunity to hone my core technical skills (while majoring in Computer Science) and still develop the Leadership skills that will help me contribute to the transformation Africa is about to experience.

I am skilled in Software development with focus on web application development. I'll be glad to receive your support and be willing to provide value to you or your organisation in return as much as I can. Thanks in anticipation.

- Daniel Osineye.
